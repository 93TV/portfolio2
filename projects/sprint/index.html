Sprint Assignment

Read me:

This is the result of a sprint assignment I did at the Odisee Technology Campus. We received a sheet with the design of the website, including pictures, text, color codes, margin & padding markups. We had 3 hours time to reconstruct this website and make it look exactly like the design we received. I actually really liked this assignment, because it gave me some self-esteem regarding HTML & CSS, I was actually capable of making it look similar. I rated myself 12 points on 20. I can still improve on many aspects of web-development and am looking forward to more challenging projects!
