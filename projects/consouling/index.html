Consouling Sounds

Read me:

At my previous job, Consouling Sounds, a Ghent based record label and shop, I had a lot of different tasks. One of them was maintaining the website and webshop. This website was a custom build website and made me think of WordPress websites where you work in a sort of template, but also the dark side of it all, endlessly searching to do small changes to it. Happily this was just a small part of my job! I like building websites from scratch and with my own design much better. Sometimes reinventing the wheel can be fun!
