Group Project

Read me:

This is the result of our group project of the first year web development course. We had no strict assignment and could choose to make any website. We got a list of competences where and needed to complete at least six of them. This is The Funraiser, with our main goal to improve quality of student life at our Odisee Campus Ghent. I added these features: a css burger menu, the javascript tilting cards on the team page, a home page with progress bar and the mission page where the articles slide and fade in while scrolling. We had a blast making this!
